Corporate Trainer · Facilitator · Leadership Coach

Dinesh Jambe

Founder

Dinesh Jambe is a dynamic Corporate Trainer, Coach, and Founder of JanNova Elements & EduTAG Academy.

With 18+ years of experience, he has trained more than 120,000 participants across 75+ organisations and institutions. His expertise lies in leadership, communication, and personal growth—blending strategy, psychology, and practical learning to inspire transformation.

Through JanNova Elements, he focuses on shaping confident, employable, and future-ready individuals, driven by his vision of “Training and Growing” people to reach their highest potential.

Mission: To optimise human potential, elevate organisations, and drive JanNova’s growth through strategic vision, integrity, and client-centric solutions.
